Scrabble

How to play:

Drag and drop the tiles from your rack into the desired board space. Once you place the first tile onto the board, all other tiles must be placed into the next available space to the right. You can remove a tile from the board and place it back on your rack. If the removal of a tile from the board results in a gap in the word, an error message will be displayed and submitting the word will be prohibited until that gap is filled. Once you have completed your desired word, click 'Submit Word' to add the word to your score and remove those letters from the "bag".
If you wish to swap out some letters in your rack, place those tiles into the swap area and click 'Swap Hand'. This will put those tiles back in the "bag" and give you new random tiles in their place. You can restart the game at any point by clicking 'Restart Game'.
